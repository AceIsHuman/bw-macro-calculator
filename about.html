How It Works

Track Calories with Macro Calculator— for FREE!

Losing weight isn't easy. But with Macro Calculator, you get the tools you need to sucessfully take weight off - and keep it off. And best of all, you can signup for free, no strings attached.

Studies have confirmed the benefits of keeping track of the food you eat and the activity you do. It's simple - the more consistently you track your food intake, the more likely you are to lose weight. That's why every successful weight management program suggests that you keep a food diary and/or an activity log. But recording everything you eat without the right tools can be tedious and time consuming.

The reason Macro Calculator is so easy to use is because it learns from you! Everyone of us tend to eat the same foods over time. Macro Calculator remembers what you've eaten and what workouts you've done most often in the past, and makes it easy for you to add those foods again to your log.

So the more you track your meals and work outs, the easier it becomes!
